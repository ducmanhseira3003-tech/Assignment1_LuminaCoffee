Our Story

Founded in 2020, Lumina Coffee started with a simple mission: to bring the purest, most authentic coffee beans directly from local farmers to your cup. We believe that every sip should tell a story of dedication, from the misty highlands to our roasting machines.

Our baristas are highly trained artisans who treat coffee-making as a form of art. Whether you are looking for a quiet corner to work or a warm space to chat with friends, Lumina is your second home.
